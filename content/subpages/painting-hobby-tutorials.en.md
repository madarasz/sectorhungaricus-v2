      ### For Beginners


      Want to start painting miniatures? Check out these awesome video tutorials:

      - [Squidmar: Ultimate guide to painting your first miniature](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S7-At4qVC84) - long, but it has tips on assembling your mini, picking the right tools, choosing paints and making cool bases

      - [Midwinter Minis: Drybrushing - the basics](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ewD7cSSRVa4) - learn drybrushing, probably the most important technique

      - [Lyla Mev: EVERY Miniature Painting Technique](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bKrvDQf_Gg8) - a variety of techniques to level up your painting

      - [Duncan Rhodes: Contrast Paints](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JJGqInWb2yo) - contrast paints can make your minis amazing with minimal efforts

      - [Sarah Renae Clark: Color Theory Basics](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YeI6Wqn4I78) - picking the right color matters

      - [Zumikito: Speedpainting space marines](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T0SCYtsRThA) - great guide for quickly painting a popular mini
